## 🛠️ How To Use

1. 🔌 **Connect your wallet** — Backpack or X1 Wallet
2. 🔢 **Enter an estimate** of how many holders you expect — this shows you the cost upfront before anything happens
3. 🔍 **Paste the mint address** of the token whose holders you want to target — RX1N will scan the X1 ledger and fetch every active holder automatically
4. 🪙 **Select the token** from your wallet you want to airdrop, choose Fungible or NFT, and enter the amount per recipient
5. 🚀 **Hit MAKE\_IT\_RX1N** — batches are processed and confirmed on-chain one by one

> 💡 If a batch fails mid-drop your progress is saved. Just hit \*\*KEEP\_RX1NING\*\* to resume exactly where you left off.

## ⚠️ Tips

* ✅ **Enable auto-sign** in your wallet to avoid manually approving every batch
* ✅ **Disable auto-lock** to prevent your wallet idling and dropping the session mid-airdrop
* ✅ Batches are groups of **7 recipients per transaction** for maximum efficiency
